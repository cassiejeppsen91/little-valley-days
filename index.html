🍽️Potluck Dinner5:30 PM | Main Street Park⌄

Join us as we celebrate America’s 250th birthday with a community potluck dinner! These America 250 Potlucks are taking place in communities across the United States, and we’re excited to bring our town together for this special occasion.

We are planning for approximately 500 participants. The town will provide the meat and drinks. We will have tables and shade tents, but you’re also welcome to bring a blanket and enjoy your meal picnic-style on the lawn.

To help ensure a variety of delicious food for everyone, please sign up to bring a dish that serves approximately 12 people.

Following the potluck dinner, we invite everyone to stay for a special patriotic program as we honor our nation’s history, and celebrate our community.

We hope you’ll join us for this memorable evening of food, fellowship, and patriotism!
